Theorem 1.3. roduct Principle (Counting by stages).

If elements of \(A\) are formed by a series of \(k\) choices where there are \(n_i\) options for the \(i^{\text{th}}\) choice, regardless of previous choices made, then
\begin{equation*} \left|{A}\right|=\prod_{i=1}^k n_i. \end{equation*}
